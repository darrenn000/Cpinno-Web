About Us

Construction Productions Innovation has been providing a wide range of precast accessories to the market since 2015. The company provides high-quality solutions including proprietary Lifting Systems, Fixing Systems, Formliners and Graphic Concrete, supported by comprehensive engineering design and technical expertise.

Our Portfolio

Our Services

JLIFE®

JLIFE offers proprietary Lifting Anchors, Fixing Systems, and Wire Loop Connection Boxes for precast construction. These solutions ensure Safe lifting, Secure fixing without post-drilling, and Reliable connections between precast elements, improving Safety and Efficiency across various building projects.

NOE & Graphic Concrete

NOE Formliners & Graphic Concrete (GC) create Natural architectural off-form concrete surfaces. NOE Formliners can be used for both cast in-situ and precast concrete projects, while Graphic Concrete is primarily used for precast concrete applications.
